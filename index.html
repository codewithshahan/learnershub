Our Specialization

  • IELTS Training

    We aimed to prepare individuals for the International English Language Testing System (IELTS) exam at no cost. This service is intended to help individuals improve their English language skills and increase their chances of achieving a desired score on the IELTS exam. So do not hesitate to visit at our office.

  • OIETC Exam

    OIETC stands for Oxford International English Test Center. It is a part of the Oxford International Education Group. For international students, it is a great alternative to IELTS. You can give OIETC online test right in our office. We help students with the four OIETC modules from first to last.

  • Interview Training

    Having a good English speaking ability is unconsciously a great way to getting excellent IELTS or OIETC score and getting a Student Visa easily. Thus, we train our clients becomming a better English speaker for OIETC, IELTS, or other Interview preparations. So, you can build up good English communication skills in no time.
